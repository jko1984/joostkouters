About Joost

Not five years ago. Right now.

I'm Joost Kouters, Director of Cybersecurity at a US AI scaleup. I carry the full security and privacy responsibility there: strategy, governance, audits (SOC 2, ISO 27001), customer negotiations, board reporting and AI security architecture.

Not a team of twenty beneath me, but the reality of most security leaders in the Netherlands: fully accountable, small team, high expectations.

I've made the journey myself, from specialist to final accountability, and I know which choices matter at which moment. The pressure, the boardroom dynamics and the dilemmas are current for me, not memory. And as one of the first AAISM-certified professionals worldwide, I help you figure out where you stand in the AI era of our profession.

Who it's for

Every rung of the ladder. Including the ones nobody talks about.

Security professionals get plenty of content support: training, certifications, no shortage of consultants. But role-specific guidance, from someone who understands your context, is rare.

A generic career coach doesn't understand why an audit finding costs you sleep, what it means to be the only person saying “no” in a commercially driven organization, or how you bring a board along without spreading fear. A peer does.
